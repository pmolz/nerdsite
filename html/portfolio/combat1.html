Spinny Boi 3000

3lb Ring Spinner

Project Overview

This robot was a collaboration between two friends and I, where we took mild inspiration from a different ring spinner from our club.

The outer ring is made of two water-jet cut pieces of 1/4in hardened steel, while the blade tip is made with a special impact-resistant AR700 steel.

Technologies Used

  • Water jet
  • 3d Printing
  • Solidworks mass analysis

Project Highlights

  • Omni-directional shuffler drive
  • 16v high current brushless motors
  • High weapon to body weight ratio




Watch our first (and last) fight here




Destruction

If you watched the video, you'll know that it only took one good impact for our robot to stop spinning up. This was caused due to a multitude of factors, namely the stripping of gears, the shattering of the inner gear ring, the burning out of one of the brushless motors, and other electronic failures.

What did survive was the shoddy omni-directional drive!
