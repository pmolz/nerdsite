Robocup Jr. Spongebot

Omni-direcional soccor robot

Project Overview

After competing in FIRST, a robotics program sponsored by LEGO, a friend and I found a robotics competition called Robocup Jr. The goal was to create two moderately sized robots that could play 2 on 2 soccer without human interaction

Unlike FIRST, there were few rules as to how the robot was built, meaning things like fancy LIDAR systems, computer vision, high power motors, etc. were all on the table

The robot you see here is not our first attempt, mind you, but serves as a great milestone in our robotics careers. (My colleague Guang-lin ended up in computer science, while I in engineering)

Technologies Used

  • Laser cutting
  • Machine Learning & Computer vision
  • PCB Design and reflow
  • Custom optical engineering
  • Omni-direcional, holonomic drive
  • 350V Pump circuit for driving solenoid kicker
  • Distance sensing for ball engagement
  • Custom molded silicone dribbler drum
